## ðŸŽ¨ DESIGN NOTES

### Color Usage
- **Primary (#0d524b - Teal):** Headers, primary buttons, navigation
- **Secondary (#ff6b2d - Orange):** CTAs, highlights, sale badges
- **Tertiary (#9ab7b4 - Light Teal):** Backgrounds, subtle accents
- **White:** Clean backgrounds, text readability
- **Dark Gray (#333):** Body text

### Typography Hierarchy
- **H1:** 48px - Hero headlines only
- **H2:** 36px - Section headlines
- **H3:** 24px - Subsections
- **Body:** 16px - Readable, comfortable spacing
- **CTA Buttons:** 18px - Bold, uppercase

### Imagery Style
- High-quality, well-lit food photography
- Show texture and freshness of products
- Include lifestyle shots (people enjoying snacks)
- Behind-the-scenes kitchen/making process
- Cultural context (traditional serving methods)

## ðŸš€ CONVERSION OPTIMIZATION TIPS

1. **Above the fold:** Hero CTA visible without scrolling
2. **Social proof:** Display review count and ratings prominently
3. **Exit-intent popup:** 10% discount offer for first-time visitors
4. **Cart abandonment:** Email reminder with incentive
5. **Product pages:** Show related products and "Frequently Bought Together"
6. **Trust signals:** Payment security badges, return policy
7. **Urgency elements:** "5 people viewing this" or "Low stock alert"
8. **Free shipping bar:** Progress indicator toward free shipping threshold
